About Us

Coffee World is an Indian multinational chain of coffeehouses and roastery reserves headquartered in Phagwara, Punjab.
It was founded in 2024 by Siddharth Raj at Law Gate, Lovely Professional University initially as a coffee bean wholesaler.
Coffee World was converted into a coffee shop serving espresso-based drinks under the ownership of Hardin Joseph,
who was chief executive officer from 2024 to 2030 and led the aggressive expansion of the franchise across the southern parts of India.
The rise of the second wave of coffee culture is generally attributed to us, which introduced a wider variety of coffee experiences.
We serve hot and cold drinks, whole-bean coffee, micro-ground instant coffee, espresso, caffe latte, full and loose-leaf teas, juices,
Frappuccino beverages, pastries, and snacks. Some offerings are seasonal or specific to the locality of the store. Depending on the country, most locations provide free Wi-Fi Internet access.
We have been subjected to multiple controversies related to its business practices. Conversely, our franchise has commanded substantial brand loyalty, market share, and company value.
